Battleships by Terry

Welcome to the digital battleground of Battleships! Command your fleet on the virtual ocean grid, outsmart the computer with precise strikes and clever tactics. Will you emerge victorious, or will the computer sink your fleet? Prepare for a quick and intense naval showdown that will test your strategic prowess. May the best tactician win!

  1. Place 5 ships in length order, 5, 4, 3, 3, 2
  2. Click the square where your ship will start, it will go across to the right or downwards*
  3. Choose from the choice of ending squares

    4. *If your ship will not fit to the right and downwards you will not be able to start it at that square

  4. Click 'Confirm Positions' button when all ships are placed
